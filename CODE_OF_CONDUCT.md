# Community Code of Conduct

Sakoon concerns prayer times, religious practice, local authorities, software,
and regional conventions. Discussions can therefore touch subjects on which
people sincerely disagree. The project asks everyone to participate with
respect and precision.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful toward people, mosques, authorities, schools of thought,
  countries, languages, and local conventions.
- Separate reproducible software behaviour from religious rulings.
- Cite the issuing authority and publication period when discussing an
  official timing table.
- Assume good faith while still asking for evidence.
- Protect personal information and remove sensitive details from logs.
- Keep bug reports focused, factual, and useful.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, safety, and public discussion.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks;
- sectarian abuse or arguments presented as software support;
- ridicule of religious practice or belief;
- impersonation of an authority or false claims of official endorsement;
- publishing private information, credentials, or sensitive diagnostics;
- knowingly submitting fabricated official tables;
- spam, commercial promotion, malware, or misleading download links;
- attempts to pressure maintainers into exposing private source code.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content; close issues or pull
requests; restrict participation; or report serious abuse to GitHub.

For conduct concerns that should not be public, contact:

`arkenapps@outlook.com`

This code applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, release
comments, and other project-managed spaces.
